Support Our Cause

Why Donate?

Your contributions help Agang SA continue its mission to empower communities, promote equality, and create positive change across South Africa.

  • Fund education and mentorship programs for youth.
  • Support community health initiatives and mobile clinics.
  • Enable campaigns for social justice and transparency.
  • Invest in skills training and economic empowerment projects.

How to Donate

You can choose a donation option that suits you. Every contribution helps us make a real impact:

  • One-Time Donation: A single contribution to support ongoing projects.
  • Monthly Contribution: Help sustain long-term initiatives with recurring support.
  • Corporate Sponsorship: Partner with us to maximize impact and reach more communities.

Donations can be made via:

  • Bank Transfer: Account details available upon request.
  • Credit/Debit Card Online Payment.
  • Mobile Payments (e.g., SnapScan, PayFast).
Donate Now

Your Impact

Every donation directly contributes to projects that make a difference:

  • R100 provides school supplies for a student for a month.
  • R500 helps run a community skills workshop.
  • R1000 supports a mobile health clinic visit in a rural area.

Thank You!

We deeply appreciate your generosity. By donating, you are joining thousands of others who are committed to building a better, fairer, and more empowered South Africa.

One Nation, One Future

Built on Freedom, Fairness, Opportunity, and Diversity for All. Agang SA is committed to creating a transparent, inclusive, and empowered South Africa.

We envision a South Africa where everyone has access to the resources, opportunities, and tools needed to thrive, contribute meaningfully to society, and achieve personal and collective success. Our vision is grounded in equity, opportunity, and social cohesion.
